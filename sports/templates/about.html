About Sports Hub

Our Story

Sports Hub is as a CS3240 Software Development project at the University of Virginia. Its mission is to provide a platform driven by our passion for sports and community building. As students who are passionate about pickup games and organized sporting, we saw an opportunity to create something personally interesting.

Our Mission

In a broader context, this application could make sporting as a whole more approachable for entire the UVA community. Sports Hub intends to connect people with fellow enthusiasts who share interests.
